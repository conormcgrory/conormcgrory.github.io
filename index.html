Research Assistant

Center for Neural Science, New York University

Hi! My name is Conor, and I’m a research assistant in Cristina Savin’s lab at NYU. I am mainly interested in the computational properties of memory: how memories are formed, encoded, and persisted over long periods of time via neural circuits.

I grew up in Falmouth, Maine (right outside of Portland), moved to New Jersey for college, and now live in Brooklyn, NY.
